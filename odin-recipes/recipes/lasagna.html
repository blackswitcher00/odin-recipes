Lasagna

Tipical italian food

Ingredients:

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes
  4. bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling wate
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover.
  7. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes
